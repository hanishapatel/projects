IELTS can be taken on Paper or Computer



Paper-based IELTS

An applicant has to solve question paper and mark the answers in the given answer sheet with a pencil for listening, reading and writing section. However, the speaking is carried out face to face with a qualified IELTS examiner
For more information visit: https://www.ielts.org/about-the-test/test-format



Computer-based IELTS

An applicant has to solve questions displayed on the screen and submit the respective answers simultaneously for listening, reading and writing section at a respective official IELTS centre while; the speaking is carried out face to face with a qualified IELTS examiner in the same format as per paper- based IELTS test.
For more information visit: https://www.ielts.org/about-the-test/test-format



Benefits of Computer-based test

  • Results in 5-7 days
  • More test availability( more days of the week)
